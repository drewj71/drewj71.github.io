About Me

I'm currently a junior at University of Michigan Dearborn pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Information Science with a concentration in Computer Science. From a young age I was always fascinated by computers. I was always interested in how things worked, this came from my father who is an electrical engineer. He would show me how to fix and engineer everything around the house, and I loved his intuitive and innovate mindset. This inspired me to want to learn everything about computers. I built my own first computer with my dad and went on to build a few more for friends. After that I discovered programming and instantly became intrigued in how to learn everything about it. I'm very passionate, motivated, and determined whenever I discover a new challenge or aspect of Computer Science.

My experience with operating systems is fluent with Windows and Mac OS. I am currently working on learning more Linux through using my RaspberryPI. The development languages that I am proficient in are C/C++, .NET Development (C#), HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Blazor, and React.
